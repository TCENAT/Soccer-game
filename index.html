🎮 Controls

⌨️ KEYBOARD CONTROLS

W - Move Forward

A - Move Left

S - Move Backward

D - Move Right

SPACE - Jump & Kick (when near ball)

E - Sprint (faster movement)

ESC - Pause Game

🎯 HOW TO PLAY

⚽ Kick Ball: Get close to the ball and press SPACE to kick it

🟡 Score Goals: Kick into the YELLOW goal zone

🎀 Unlock Accessories: Score 3, 6, and 10 goals to unlock items

⏱️ Time Limit: You have 2 minutes per game

🏆 Difficulty: Higher levels = More NPCs & Faster opponents
